So for this high octane blast of randomastrous, we’re going to get into something a bit silly. I grew up with a mom who would rehabilitate injured wild animals as a sort of hobby I guess. We always had animals around anyway. So things like waking up at 5am to baby raccoons crying downstairs and the sight of a baby skunk up close without all of that notorious shyness were somewhat the norm. Bottle feeding baby animals was a bit of a pain to be honest, it was that common, just one of the chores. So, in an ode to my mom’s kindness and the greedy yet somehow cute nature of baby animals. Here’s ANIMALS ON THE BOTTLE
